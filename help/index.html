Frequently asked

How does the “cover ABN” thing work?

When your organisation covers a sub-contractor's ABN access, their Ozly subscription is paid through your seat fee — they don't pay anything. In return, their invoices on Ozly are limited to your organisation only. If they want to also bill other clients, they can add a $5/month personal top-up in the app (still on your cover).

Can a sub-contractor be in more than one organisation?

Yes. They can be a member of multiple orgs. The org-portal selector in their app lets them pick which one to bill. The "cover ABN" lock applies to the union of all covering orgs.

What happens at the end of the 14-day trial?

If you've added a payment method, billing starts automatically at your active tier (auto-detected from your accepted member count). If not, your members lose ABN cover after a 7-day grace period. You can re-subscribe at any time and they pick up where they left off.

How do I export invoices for BAS / quarterly tax?

In the Org Portal: Reports → BAS quarterly. Pick the fiscal year (Jul→Jun) and quarter, then Export CSV. The columns map directly to the ATO portal fields. The free CSV export covers most BAS lodgements; the Xero / MYOB integration (Phase 3) auto-pushes the same data.

How do I upgrade or downgrade my tier?

Only the org owner can change tiers (admins are read-only on Billing). Go to Billing → pick the new tier → confirm. Upgrades are immediate with Stripe-prorated billing. Downgrades go through a brief exit-interview flow and take effect at the next renewal.

How do I send an invoice directly to an org from the app?

When creating an invoice, pick the org from the "Your companies" group in the Bill-to picker. The "Send directly to org" toggle is on by default — confirm and emit. The PDF goes straight to the org's inbox in the portal. Tick "Email me a copy" to also receive it.

What's the difference between “ABN” and “PRO”?

ABN access = invoice your clients with your ABN on every invoice. PRO = ABN + tax tools (visa-conditional Medicare Levy, fiscal year planner). Both are $14.99/mo and $19.99/mo respectively when paying individually. Org-cover users can add $5 or $9 top-ups to upgrade incrementally.

What if a member leaves the org?

Their membership is marked “removed”. Your seat count drops automatically at the next billing cycle. Their personal Ozly app continues working with whatever billing they had before joining you — your data flow stops, theirs continues.

How is Ozly compliant with Fair Work / ATO?

Ozly never creates an employment relationship. All language, retention, and behaviour is built around the sub-contractor/engagement framework. We don't capture TFNs, we don't sign engagement contracts, and we don't dictate work patterns. See our Business Terms for the full clause.
