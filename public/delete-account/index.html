Delete Your Ozly Account & Data

Request the permanent deletion of your Ozly account and every piece of personal data we hold about you.

Warning: Once your account is deleted, all your data (profile, jobs, invoices, expenses, receipts, and business records) will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

When we process your deletion request, the following is permanently erased from Ozly's servers and backups:

  • Your profile details (name, email, phone number, ABN, and business information).
  • All jobs, quotes, and client records.
  • Every invoice, payment, and tax record you created inside Ozly.
  • All expense entries, categories, and receipt images.
  • Contractor records and any shared business data tied to your account.
  • Authentication tokens, device registrations, and push notification subscriptions.

How long it takes

Ozly processes deletion requests within 30 days of receipt, in line with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles. A confirmation email is sent to the address you used to sign up once your account has been removed. Backups are purged on a rolling 90-day cycle.

Before you go — alternatives

If you no longer want to pay for Pro features, you can cancel your subscription from the App Store or Google Play without deleting your data. If you simply want a break, you can sign out and come back later. Deletion is permanent and irreversible, so make sure you have exported any invoices or receipts you may need for tax purposes.

Delete directly from the app

You can also delete your Ozly account straight from the mobile app without filling in this form. Open the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account, and confirm the action. The in-app deletion flow uses the same 30-day processing window described above.
