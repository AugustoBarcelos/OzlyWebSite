Delete Account & Data

How to permanently delete your Ozly account and all associated data.

Your data, your control. Ozly allows you to delete your account and all personal data at any time. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Option 1 — Delete from the App

The quickest way to delete your account:

  1. Open the Ozly app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap "Delete Account".
  4. Type DELETE to confirm.

Your account and all data will be removed immediately.

Option 2 — Request via Email

If you can no longer access the app, you can request account deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to privacy@ozly.au.
  2. Use the same email address associated with your Ozly account.
  3. Include the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm once complete.

What Data Is Deleted?

The following data is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our servers:

  • Your user profile and authentication credentials.
  • All work hours, jobs, and associated calendar links.
  • All invoices and invoice line items.
  • All expenses and uploaded receipt images.
  • All contractor/client records.
  • All business records (ABN, banking details).
  • All analytics events associated with your account.

This action cannot be undone. Local data on your device is also cleared upon sign-out.

Important: Cancel Your Subscription

Deleting your Ozly account does not automatically cancel your App Store or Google Play subscription. To avoid further charges, cancel your subscription separately:

  • iPhone/iPad: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Ozly → Cancel.
  • Android: Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → Ozly → Cancel.
